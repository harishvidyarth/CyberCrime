📘 Fund Flow Graph — Legend

Node markers

  • 🔁 Repeater: Multiple transactions between the same two accounts.
  • 🏧 ATM: Funds withdrawn from this account via ATM.
  • 🎫 Cheque: Funds withdrawn from this account via cheque.
  • 🔒 Hold: Funds at this account are currently frozen (on hold).
  • 💥 Burst: Account forwards funds to 20+ accounts; auto-expansion is disabled for performance.
  • Repeated account: Same account recurs in 2+ places of the trail (possible mule). Use the Repeated Accounts button to find them.

Interactions

  • Single click: Expand or collapse the account's onward transfers.
  • Double click: Open the account's full KYC & transaction details.
  • Icon click: Open the ATM / Cheque / Hold details for that node.
